🌿 Olmi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Olmi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Olmi app (available on iOS).

By using Olmi, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Name and email address — when you create an account or sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Plant data — the plants you add, care logs, and reminders you create within the app.
  • Purchase information — subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To sync your plant data across sessions.
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset).
  • To manage your subscription and access to premium features.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management. Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign In — optional authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign In — optional authentication. Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Profile → Delete Account) or by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Olmi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure token storage, and hashed passwords to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
